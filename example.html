About Save the Penguins

Thanks for visiting Save the Penguins! Antartica is being hit hard by the effects of global climate change. The Antartic Peninsula is warming faster than almost anywhere on Earth. When it is warmer, it rains. When it rains, penguin chicks die.

What You Can Do

You can help by taking the bus, insulating your house, and other really non-sexy things to reduce carbon emissions.
